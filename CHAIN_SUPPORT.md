# Chain Support Roadmap

This document outlines the current and planned blockchain network integrations for Jupiter Sentinel.

## Solana
* **Integration Status:** Done
* **Available Protocols:** Jupiter, Raydium, Orca, Meteora
* **Average Gas Costs:** Very Low (< $0.01)
* **Expected Opportunities:** High-frequency arbitrage, micro-scalping, meme coin volatility, liquid staking arbitrage.

## Polygon (PoS)
* **Integration Status:** In Progress
* **Available Protocols:** Uniswap V3, QuickSwap, Balancer, Aave
* **Average Gas Costs:** Low (~$0.01 - $0.05)
* **Expected Opportunities:** Cross-DEX arbitrage, triangular arbitrage, yield farming aggregation.

## Ethereum (Mainnet)
* **Integration Status:** Planned
* **Available Protocols:** Uniswap V2/V3, SushiSwap, Curve, Balancer, 1inch
* **Average Gas Costs:** High ($2.00 - $50.00+)
* **Expected Opportunities:** MEV (Miner Extractable Value) searcher strategies, flash loan arbitrage, large-cap liquidity provision, stablecoin peg arbitrage. Requires significant capital to offset gas.

## Base
* **Integration Status:** Planned
* **Available Protocols:** Aerodrome, Uniswap V3, BaseSwap
* **Average Gas Costs:** Low (~$0.05 - $0.15)
* **Expected Opportunities:** Emerging ecosystem arbitrage, new token launches, concentrated liquidity provision.

## Arbitrum (One)
* **Integration Status:** Planned
* **Available Protocols:** Uniswap V3, Camelot, SushiSwap, Trader Joe
* **Average Gas Costs:** Low (~$0.10 - $0.30)
* **Expected Opportunities:** Cross-rollup arbitrage (vs Optimism/Base), perpetual DEX funding rate arbitrage (e.g., GMX), concentrated liquidity strategies.
